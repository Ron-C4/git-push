2022 Ticket Plans

The 1-Day GA Ticket allows you admittance to Psyched Summer Fest for one day of your choice. One-Day GA Perks include:
  • Acess to all music stages and comedy tent, food & beverage vendors, and one-of-a-kind experiences to explore.
  • Acess to Merch Vendor Tents
  • Cost: $150

The 3-Day GA Ticket allows you admittance to Psyched Summer Fest for all three days of the festival. Three-Day GA Perks include:
  • Access to all music stages and comedy tent, food & beverage vendors, and one-of-a-kind experiences to explore.
  • Access to Merch Vendor Tents
  • Free Shuttle Pass to Venue
  • Optional Payment Plan Available
  • Cost: $350

The 3-Day VIP Ticket allows you admittance to Psyched Summer Fest for all three days of the festival, in addition to several special perks. Three-Day VIP Perks include:
  • Acess to all music stages and comedy tent, food & beverage vendors, and one-of-a-kind experiences to explore.
  • Priority Access to Merch Vendor Tents
  • Free 2022 Psyched Summer Fest Merch
  • Free Shuttle Pass to Venue or Valet Parking
  • Access to special Psyched Summer VIP Lounges
  • Food and Beer Garden Vouchers
  • Access to VIP Entrance
  • Access Front row VIP Areas
  • Cost: $750
